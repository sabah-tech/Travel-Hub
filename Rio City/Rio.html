Discover Rio

capital of the estado (state) of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Often referred to as the 'Cidade Maravilhosa' or 'Marvellous City,'

Welcome to Rio

Rio de Janeiro is a huge seaside city in Brazil, famed for its Copacabana and Ipanema beaches, 38m Christ the Redeemer statue atop Mount Corcovado and for Sugarloaf Mountain, a granite peak with cable cars to its summit.

Top Attraction Places in Rio

Christ the Redeemer

Christ the Redeemer is an Art Deco statue of Jesus in Rio de Janeiro,Brazil, created by French-Polish sculptor Paul Landowski

Escadaria Selarón

Escadaria Selarón, locally known as the 'Lapa Steps' (Escadaria da Lapa) for being situated at the "Lapa" neighborhood

stadium

A purpose-built stadium constructed specially to host the annual parade of Samba Schools each year during the festival of Carnaval in Rio de Janeiro,Brazil.

The Quinta

The Quinta da Boa Vista is a public park of great historical importance located in the São Cristóvão neighbourhood, in the North zone of the city of Rio

Parque Lage

A day in Parque Henrique Lage that is public park in the city of Rio de Janeiro, located in the Jardim Botânico neighborhood at the foot of the Corcovado, on top of which Christ the Redeemer is located.

Museu do Amanhã

A day in The Museum of Tomorrow that is new kind of Science museum. Here you are invited to examine the past, understand today's global trends and envision possible

Christ by Train

Corcovado train up to the Christ the Redeemer statue, take a cable car ride up to the Sugarloaf Mountain, see the famed Maracana soccer stadium

Jeep Adventure

Ride through Tijuca National Park's rainforest in an open-top Jeep during this half-day trip from Rio. During guided eco-tour, hike through the forest to spot sloths, butterflies, monkeys, and birds.

Experience Hang Gliding

Janeiro from a whole new angle during a private tour that offers the option of hang gliding or paragliding over the Eternal City

Food in Rio

Try Feijoada

feijoada is a dish made from beans, very similar to “Cassoulet” in France, “Cozido” in Portugal, “Casoeula” in Italy, and “Puchero” in Spain

Taste Barbecue steakhouse

known worldwide for offering quality meat. And Cariocas do live up to this fame! Although Rio de Janeiro is not a region specializing in beef cattle, several restaurants serve excellent meat throughout the city

Explore Oswaldo Aranha Fillet

consists of a piece of filet mignon or fillet seasoned with fried garlic

Additional Information for Your Rio Trip

Weather in Rio

Rio has a tropical savanna climate (Aw) that closely borders a tropical monsoon climate (Am) according to the Köppen climate classification

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Rio is between December and March, when the weather is warm and sunny enough to hit the beaches

Local Etiquette

  • Brazilians always wash their hands before eating and rarely touch food with their hands.
  • Always use a napkin while eating or drinking.
  • Don't plan a big Sunday shopping trip.

Currency and Payment Methods

a mixture of credit / debit cards and cash is best to take with you

Emergency Numbers

  • Police: 190
  • Ambulance : 192
  • Fire: 193

Transportation Tips

  • Buy transport tickets at official sales points.
  • Keep your ticket until exiting the transport system.
  • Opt for official transport for peace of mind.
  • Beware of fake inspectors and tickets.
