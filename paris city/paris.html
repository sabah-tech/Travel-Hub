Welcome to Paris

Paris, the capital and largest city of France; an international center of culture and commerce. Known as the City of Love, the Fashion Capital, and the City of Lights. Additionally, it is one of the world's major centers of finance, diplomacy, commerce, culture, fashion, and gastronomy.

Top Attraction Places in Paris

Eiffel Tower

one of the most iconic landmarks in the world, is located in Paris At the time, it was the tallest man-made structure in the world.

palace Of Versailles

the things that make palace of versailles famous is the Magnificent Architecture and Art, Gardens of Versailles, Grand Trianon and Petit Trianon and the Historical Significance.

lourve Museum

The Louvre Museum in Paris is the world’s largest art museum it renowned for its vast collection of art and artifacts The Louvre is not only a center of artistic heritage but also a bustling hub for visitors.

Paris Disnyland

Disneyland Paris is one of Europe’s most-visited tourist destinations, drawing millions of guests each year Recently, it has been undergoing a major expansion, with new lands dedicated to Marvel, Star Wars, and Frozen to enhance its offerings and attract even more visitors.

Additional Information for Your Paris Trip

Weather in Paris

Paris has a typical oceanic climate with mild, moderately wet weather year-round. Summer temperatures range between 15-25°C (59-77°F).

Best Time to Visit

The best times to visit Paris are April, early May, September, and early October.

Local Etiquette

  • Do say "bonjour" and keep your voice down.
  • Explore beyond the Left Bank.
  • Don't plan a big Sunday shopping trip.

Currency and Payment Methods

Visa and Mastercard are widely accepted in France.

Emergency Numbers

  • Police: 17
  • Ambulance & Fire: 112
  • Tourist Info Center: 15
  • Medical Emergency (English): 18

Transportation Tips

  • Buy transport tickets at official sales points.
  • Keep your ticket until exiting the transport system.
  • Opt for official transport for peace of mind.
  • Beware of fake inspectors and tickets.
