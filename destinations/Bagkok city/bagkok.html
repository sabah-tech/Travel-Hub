Welcome to Bangkok

Bangkok, Thailand’s capital, is a large city known for ornate shrines and vibrant street life. The boat-filled Chao Phraya River feeds its network of canals, flowing past the Rattanakosin royal district, home to opulent Grand Palace and its sacred Wat Phra Kaew Temple.

Top Attraction Places in Bangkok

Grand Palace

The Grand Palace is a complex of buildings at the heart of Bangkok, Thailand. The palace has been the official residence of the Kings of Siam

THE JIM THOMPSON HOUSE MUSEUM.

The Bangkok home and Asian art collection of the late James H.W. Thompson, also known as the "ThaiSilk King”

Wat Traimit Witthayaram

Wat Traimit Witthayaram Worawihan is a Theravada Buddhist temple in Samphanthawong district, the Chinatown area of the The capital Bangkok

Wat Arun Ratchawaramahawihan

Wat Arun Ratchawaramahawihan or Wat Arun is a Buddhist temple (wat) in the Bangkok Yai district of Bangkok, Thailand.

Additional Information for Your Bangkok Trip

Weather in Paris

The city of Bangkok, the capital of Thailand, is renowned for its humid tropical climate.

Best Time to Visit

November to February is the best time for comfortable weather.

Local Etiquette

  • Show Respect to the Royal Family.
  • Dress Modestly.
  • Use Both Hands When Giving or Receiving Something.

Currency and Payment Methods

cash will likely be the only option.

  • Police:191

Transportation Tips

  • Buy transport tickets at official sales points.
  • Keep your ticket until exiting the transport system.
  • Opt for official transport for peace of mind.
  • Beware of fake inspectors and tickets.
